    Welcome to the official website of the Department of Chemistry at Ramkrishna Mission Vivekananda Centenary College, Rahara. This site is designed and developed by Avishek Das, a passionate developer committed to making academic resources more accessible for students and educators alike. This platform serves as a hub for the students of the Department of Chemistry, providing essential study materials, faculty information, and other academic resources. It aims to enhance the learning experience by offering a user-friendly interface and easy access to important notes, model question papers, and faculty contacts. Thank you for visiting, and we hope this website serves as a valuable tool in your academic journey. If you have any suggestions or feedback, feel free to reach out!
